Research

Currently, there exists no automated technology to diagnose a patient with leukemia just by analyzing the peripheral blood smear obtained from a blood test. Our research focuses around the idea of designing a Machine Learning model that would be trained on existing data set to verify cancerous blood cells from a blood smear, ultimately automating the process of detecting leukemia, and reducing the time delay currently present in the diagnosis process by experts
